Ensuring Food Safety & Traceability

At Agro Food Safety, we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards in food safety and traceability. Our team of professionals works tirelessly across each sector, ensuring that every step of the journey – from farm to retailer – upholds our commitment to quality. With a robust workforce including inspectors, storage and logistics personnel, and processing experts, we prioritize excellence at every level.

Farm

Our journey begins at the farm, where we partner with farmers to implement sustainable and safe agricultural practices. Food inspectors conduct regular checks to ensure quality right from the start.

Processing Center

In processing centers, rigorous safety measures and quality control processes are in place. We ensure that food products meet our strict standards before moving forward in the supply chain.

Storage

Proper storage is crucial for maintaining food safety and freshness. Our storage facilities are equipped with temperature control and security systems to preserve product integrity.

Transport

Our transport team ensures that food products are delivered safely and efficiently to retailers. Our inspectors monitor transportation conditions to minimize risks along the way.

Retailer

Finally, our network of retailers brings safe food to consumers. We ensure that all products on shelves are fully traceable and meet our quality standards, providing confidence to every buyer.

Our Workforce

Agro Food Safety employs over 5,000 dedicated professionals across various sectors:

  • 1,200 Inspectors: Ensure compliance and safety from farm to retailer.
  • 1,500 Processing Center Workers: Oversee the production process with rigorous quality controls.
  • 800 Storage Personnel: Maintain optimal conditions for food safety.
  • 900 Logistics and Transport Staff: Safely transport products across regions.
  • 600 Retail Support Members: Facilitate distribution and traceability at retail locations.
Together, they uphold our mission of delivering safe, high-quality food to every consumer.
